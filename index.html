Who I am

I am Michiel van Es, a guitar player with a passion for overdrive pedals, pickups and simplicity.

Since I was a young boy I always loved the sound of hard rock and metal music and especially the sound of a powerchord over a high gain amp.

I learned playing guitar when I was 14 and played in bands later on.

I too had issues of G.A.S. (Gear Aqcuiring Syndome) and found out that a heavy guitar sound can be improved by the use of OD pedals and good pickups with a lot of output and clarity.

One more thing

I create overdrive pedals because I love how they make your distorted sound of your amp even better.

My pedals are not better or look amazing like some boutique pedals, I just create OD pedals with simplicity in mind; an on/off switch and apply some mods (for example Keeley Mods) and replace the IC chip with something I or you like better then the common chipset.

'Better' is something personal, something you as guitar player can decide for yourself.

I started with the known TS 808 sound and compared my pedals to famous OD-808 pedals and modded my pedal to sound better to my ears by having a tigher bass, more output and more clarity.

I test my pedals over different high gain amps like the EVH 5150 III, Peavey 6505, Framus Cobra, ENGL Fireball, Mesa Boogie Electra Dyne and see how the pedals compare to the more known OD-808 pedals and in many or perhaps even all cases my pedals sound better to my ears then the better known OD pedals.

The pedals are also tested by other guitar players who can attest that the pedals sounds better to their ears.

You might like the regular OD or TS-808 pedals that are out there or like pedals that you can tweak to death, please buy those and be happy.

I think my pedals sound 'better' and are easier to use with just a 'On/Off' knob and do their job very well.

I believe in K.I.S.S. - Keep It Simple Stupid - the pedal should be turned on or off and enhance your sound, no extra buttons needed or tweaks possible - just on and off and enjoy your punch :)
